About PushCheck

The accountability app your friend group actually needs

What is PushCheck?

PushCheck is a push-up accountability app designed specifically for friend groups. We help you track your push-ups, challenge your friends, and see who actually shows up.

Our Mission

We believe accountability works better with friends. Talking about working out in the group chat is easy. Actually doing it? That's different. PushCheck makes it visible who's showing up and who's making excuses.

Core Features

  • Friend Group Challenges: Create groups, invite friends, compete on shared leaderboards
  • Real-Time Leaderboards: See exactly who's showing up and who's falling behind
  • Daily Push-Up Tracking: Simple, fast logging of your push-ups
  • Streak Tracking: Build consistency with daily streaks visible to your friends
  • Social Accountability: Your friends can see your progress (or lack thereof)
  • Progress Statistics: Daily, weekly, monthly, and all-time stats
  • Multiple Groups: Join work friends, gym crew, or multiple challenges at once

What Makes PushCheck Different?

Most push-up apps focus on solo training plans or complex AI counting. PushCheck is laser-focused on friend-group accountability. We're not trying to be everything for everyone. We're the app for friend groups who want to do push-ups together and see who actually shows up.

Key Differentiators:

  • Friend-First: Designed for groups, not solo users
  • Simple: No complex training plans, just push-ups and accountability
  • Free: No ads, no premium tiers, no hidden costs
  • Honest: Manual entry because we trust you (and your friends hold you accountable)
  • Clean: No clutter, no BS, just what matters

Who PushCheck Is For

PushCheck is for friend groups who:

  • Talk about working out but struggle with consistency
  • Want real accountability, not just solo tracking
  • Thrive on friendly competition
  • Prefer simple apps over overcomplicated fitness platforms
  • Actually want to see who shows up

Privacy & Data

Your push-up data is only visible to friends you explicitly add to your groups. We don't sell your data, show ads, or share your stats publicly. What happens in your friend group stays in your friend group.
